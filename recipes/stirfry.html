Stir Fry Recipe

Description

This recipe is also for if you have no plans for tonight since its so insanely easy.

Essentially, you just cook some rice, cook some chicken and your preference of vegetables. Then you just combine then with some homemade teriyaki sauce. This shouldn't take more than an hour.

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 3 chicken breasts
  • Some smoked paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Kosher Salt
  • Pepper

Rice

  • 1 1/2 cups rice
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • Kosher salt

Teriyaki Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven 375.
  2. Season chicken and place in oven covered with foil for 25-30 minutes
  3. Rinse rice and cook using rice cooker
  4. For the sauce, whisk water, soy sauce, sugar, pineapple juice, vegetable oil, garlic, and ginger until sugar has dissolved.
  5. Boil sauce to reduce it
  6. Add cornstarch and mater mixture to thicken it.
  7. Take chicken out let it rest and then cut into cubes.
  8. Add butter and salt to rice and combine to make stirfry.
  9. Enjoy.
