Buttermilk Chicken Tender Recipe

Description

A wonderful recipe shared by my girfriend and I usually when we have no other plans for dinner that night

Including time for chicken to marinate, this recipe shouldn't take longer than 5 hours. At least 4 hours for marinate and 20 minutes to fry the chicken.

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 2 pounds chicken tenderloins
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika

Breading

  • 1 1/2 cup AP flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 heaping teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 3 tablespoons buttermilk

Steps

  1. In a large sealable bag, combine the chicken tenders with the marinade. Seal the bag tightly. Massage the chicken.
  2. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or up to 24 hours.
  3. In a large bowl combine flour, baking powder, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  4. Whisk until well blended
  5. Add buttermilk and stir with fork until the mixture is evenly clumpy.
  6. Line a baking sheet with aluminium foil.
  7. Remove the chicken tenders from the marinade and toss in the breading mixture.
  8. Set breaded tenders on the baking sheet.
  9. Add 3 to 4 cups of vegetable oil to a large, high-sided pot.
  10. Heat over medium to high heat (around 350 degrees Faherenheit)
  11. Using tongs, place chicken tenders in the hot oil without crowded the pan.
  12. Cook until golden brown, around 3 minutes on each side.
  13. Serve and enjoy.
